A composition

Four years in the making. NOIRE is the work of perfumer Élise Marchand, composed at her atelier in Grasse from a short list of rare materials. Each bottle is filled, sealed and numbered by hand — never repeated, never duplicated.

The Pyramid

Three movements,
one shadow.

— 01 —

Top

Bergamot & Pink Pepper

A first breath, citric and bright, cut with the warmth of pink peppercorn. The shadow has not yet fallen.

— 02 —

Heart

Iris & Black Rose

Powdered iris meets a darkened rose absolute. The fragrance turns inward — quieter, richer, unmistakably feminine.

— 03 —

Base

Oud, Amber & Vetiver

Cambodian oud anchored by black amber and Haitian vetiver. A trail that lingers for hours, low and warm.
